Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onion, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday cupcake!

Ingredients

    For the Focaccia:
  • 4 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil
    For the Topping:
  • 3 yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
